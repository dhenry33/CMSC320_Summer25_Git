
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git helps managing changes and lets you see the history of the changes. It also provides an effective way to manage collaboration with other coders. It also allows an effective way to backup your code.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: It can be applied to almost any field. For example, risk analysis in finance.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging just combines the histories of the two branches by making a new merge commit. This preserves the entire history of both branches. However rebasing moves the branchs commits on top of the other branch. Rebasing creates a linear history by moving the branch onto the top of main branch.
